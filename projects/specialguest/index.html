Intellectual Curiosity Makes the Future Better

Yobinori Takumi

Education-related YouTuber

Profile
He received his M.S. from the University of Tokyo. He majored in theoretical physics when he was a student. When entering the doctoral course, he quit his job as a teacher at a prep school, which he had been doing for 6 years, and started a YouTube channel called "Yobinori," or "Learning University Mathematics and Physics in the mood of a Prep School" as part of his science outreach activities. The channel offers videos of lessons on college-level math, physics, and other science subjects, and has 1 million subscribers.

Lecture Title : Intellectual Curiosity Makes the Future Better
