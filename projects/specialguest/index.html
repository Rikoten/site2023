Special Guest

Paying off the foreshadowing of intellectual curiosity

YOBINORI-Takumi

Educational YouTuber

Profile
Master Degree at the University of Tokyo. During undergraduate course, he majored in theoretical physics. When he entered the doctoral program, he quit his job as a preparatory school instructor for six years and opened a YouTube channel, ``Learn University Mathematics and Physics in a Preparatory School Style'' (commonly known as Yobinori) as part of his science outreach activities. He distributes videos of university-level science subjects, mainly mathematics and physics. 1 million subscriber

Go to YouTube Channel

Lecture Title：Paying off the foreshadowing of intellectual curiosity

In this lecture, I will talk about the appeal and depth of science and engineering while looking back on my own life. Looking back, various events held important keys in my life. If you are worried about your future or want to be motivated to study, please come and listen!
