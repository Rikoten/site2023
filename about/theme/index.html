"活気"あふれる"一瞬の"特別な日となること、

Theme

70th Rikoten Theme

This year's theme is "Spark".

The theme is hoping that everyone
involved in Rikoten
will have an "instantaneous" special day
filled with "shine" and "vitality",
and that Rikoten will be an "opportunity"
for new discoveries and ideas.
